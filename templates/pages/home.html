What we do ?

Jumahes is a programming school. For us learn to code is not about vocation but a required skill in order to understand how our world works.

Why learn to code?

Computers are part of our everyday life, we use them to surf the internet, do a Google search, see pictures, read e-mails, play games or write text. But how they do all of this? Is it magic? Yes, it's magic. A magic called coding or programming.

Positive aspects

Programming is an activity that:

  • Stimulates creativity
  • Promotes collaboration
  • Develops rigor, patience and perseverance
  • It trains a logical thinking to solve problems
